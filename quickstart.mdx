Get up and running with Deni AI in under 2 minutes. This guide walks you through creating an account and starting your first conversation with advanced AI models.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Visit [Deni AI](https://deniai.app) and click **Get Started** to create your account.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="OAuth (Recommended)">
        Sign up instantly with your existing account:

        - **Google**: Use your Google account for quick access
        - **GitHub**: Perfect for developers - sign in with GitHub

        Click your preferred provider, authorize the connection, and you're in. No password to remember.

        <Tip>
          OAuth providers offer the fastest signup experience and include automatic email verification.
        </Tip>
      </Tab>

      <Tab title="Email & Password">
        Create an account with your email address:

        1. Enter your email and create a password
        2. Check your inbox for a verification email
        3. Click the verification link to activate your account
        4. Sign in with your credentials

        <Info>
          Email verification is required for security. The link expires in 1 hour.
        </Info>

        **Password requirements:**
        - Passwords are checked against the Have I Been Pwned database
        - Choose a strong, unique password
        - You can reset it anytime via email
      </Tab>

      <Tab title="Magic Link">
        Sign in without a password using magic links:

        1. Enter your email address
        2. Check your inbox for a sign-in link
        3. Click the link to access your account instantly

        <Note>
          Magic links expire after one use and are perfect for quick, secure access without managing passwords.
        </Note>
      </Tab>

      <Tab title="Passkey">
        Use biometric authentication with passkeys (most secure):

        - Face ID, Touch ID, or Windows Hello
        - Device-based authentication
        - No passwords to remember or manage

        <Warning>
          Passkey setup requires a supported device with biometric capabilities. You can add passkeys after signing up with another method via **Settings**.
        </Warning>
      </Tab>

      <Tab title="Guest Mode">
        Try Deni AI without creating an account:

        1. Click **Continue as Guest** on the sign-in page
        2. Start chatting immediately with anonymous access

        <Warning>
          Guest mode limitations:
          - Conversations are temporary and tied to your browser session
          - No access to team features or advanced settings
          - Limited usage compared to registered accounts

          We recommend creating a free account to save your conversations and unlock all features.
        </Warning>
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your AI model">
    After signing in, you'll see the chat interface with a model selector. Deni AI offers access to cutting-edge models from multiple providers:

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="OpenAI Models" icon="openai">
        **GPT-5.2** — Latest flagship model with advanced reasoning, smart responses, and fast performance

        **GPT-5.3 Codex** — Specialized for coding tasks with deep programming knowledge

        **GPT-5.1 Codex & Codex Mini** — Faster coding assistants for quick code help

        **GPT-oss 120b & 20b** — Open-source models via Groq for ultra-fast responses
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Anthropic Models" icon="anthropic">
        **Claude Opus 4.6** (Pro) — The most capable Claude model for complex reasoning

        **Claude Opus 4.5** (Pro) — Previous flagship with excellent performance

        **Claude Sonnet 4.5** (Pro) — Balanced model offering great speed and intelligence

        <Note>
          Anthropic models marked with "Pro" may require a premium subscription or have usage limits on free accounts.
        </Note>
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Google Models" icon="google">
        **Gemini 3 Pro** — Google's smartest model with multimodal capabilities

        **Gemini 3 Flash** — Fast responses with strong reasoning abilities

        **Gemini 2.5 Flash Lite** — Lightweight model for quick queries
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="xAI Models" icon="x-twitter">
        **Grok 4.1 Fast (Reasoning)** — Fast model with built-in reasoning capabilities

        **Grok 4.1 Fast (Non-Reasoning)** — Faster responses without explicit reasoning steps

        **Grok Code Fast 1** — Specialized for coding and technical tasks
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="OpenRouter" icon="route">
        **Pony Alpha** — Reasoning-focused model from the OpenRouter ecosystem
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>

    <Tip>
      **Not sure which model to choose?**
      - For general questions: **GPT-5.2** or **Gemini 3 Pro**
      - For coding: **GPT-5.3 Codex** or **Grok Code Fast 1**
      - For speed: **GPT-oss 20b** or **Gemini 3 Flash**
      - For complex reasoning: **Claude Opus 4.6** or **GPT-5.2**
    </Tip>

    You can switch models at any time during your conversation using the model selector button (press **Ctrl/Cmd + K** for quick access).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start your first conversation">
    Now you're ready to chat! Here's how to make the most of your first conversation:

    ### Send your first message

    Type your question or prompt in the chat composer at the bottom of the screen and press **Enter** to send.

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Try these prompts" icon="lightbulb">
        - "Explain quantum computing in simple terms"
        - "Write a Python function to parse JSON"
        - "Help me plan a trip to Japan"
        - "Review this code for bugs: [paste code]"
      </Card>
      <Card title="Use suggestions" icon="sparkles">
        Click any of the suggestion cards on the home screen:
        - Creative Writing
        - Code Help
        - Summarize
        - Analyze

        These are pre-written prompts to help you get started quickly.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    ### Advanced options

    Before sending your message, you can configure these options:

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Web Search">
        Toggle **Web Search** to let the AI access real-time information from the web:

        - Current events and news
        - Latest documentation and guides
        - Real-time data and statistics
        - Recent developments in any field

        When enabled, the AI will search the web using Brave Search API and cite its sources.

        <Info>
          Web search results appear in a dedicated section below the AI's response with clickable source links.
        </Info>
      </Tab>

      <Tab title="File Attachments">
        Click the **attachment** button to upload files:

        - **Images**: For vision-capable models to analyze photos, screenshots, diagrams
        - **Documents**: Share code files, text documents, or other content
        - **Multiple files**: Attach multiple files to a single message

        <Warning>
          File support varies by model. Vision-capable models like GPT-5, Gemini 3, and Claude Opus can analyze images.
        </Warning>
      </Tab>

      <Tab title="Image Generation">
        Enable **Image Mode** to generate images from your text prompts:

        - Describe what you want to see
        - The AI will generate a custom image
        - Download generated images with metadata
        - View resolution, aspect ratio, and model info

        <Tip>
          Be specific in your image prompts: "A serene mountain lake at sunset with pine trees in the foreground" works better than "a lake".
        </Tip>
      </Tab>

      <Tab title="Video Generation">
        Enable **Video Mode** for models that support video generation:

        - Create videos from text descriptions
        - Playback with full controls
        - Download generated videos
        - View technical metadata (resolution, duration, seed)

        <Note>
          Video generation is only available with select models and may have longer processing times.
        </Note>
      </Tab>

      <Tab title="Reasoning Effort">
        For reasoning models, adjust the **Reasoning Effort** level:

        - **Low**: Faster responses with basic reasoning
        - **Medium**: Balanced reasoning and speed
        - **High**: Deep reasoning for complex problems

        The AI's thought process is displayed in a collapsible section showing internal reasoning steps.

        <Info>
          Higher reasoning effort may result in slower responses but better accuracy for complex tasks.
        </Info>
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

    ### Watch the AI respond

    As the AI generates its response, you'll see:

    - **Real-time streaming**: Text appears as it's being generated
    - **Thinking indicator**: Shimmer effect while processing
    - **Chain of thought**: Reasoning steps (for capable models)
    - **Source citations**: Links to web sources (when web search is enabled)

    You can **stop generation** at any time by clicking the stop button.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Manage your conversations">
    Your conversations are automatically saved as you chat. Here's how to manage them:

    ### Conversation sidebar

    - **View history**: Access all past conversations sorted by recent activity
    - **Search**: Find specific conversations quickly
    - **Organize**: Conversations are auto-titled based on content
    - **Rename**: Click any conversation title to rename it

    ### Message actions

    Each AI response includes helpful actions:

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Regenerate" icon="arrows-rotate">
        Don't like the response? Click regenerate to get a new one with the same settings.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Copy" icon="copy">
        Copy any message to your clipboard with one click.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Download" icon="download">
        Save generated images or videos directly from the chat.
      </Card>
      <Card title="View sources" icon="link">
        See all web sources cited in the response.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    ### Delete conversations

    Remove conversations you no longer need:
    - Hover over a conversation in the sidebar
    - Click the delete icon
    - Confirm deletion

    <Warning>
      Deleted conversations cannot be recovered. Make sure to save any important information before deleting.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for great conversations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Be specific with your prompts" icon="bullseye">
    The more specific you are, the better the AI can help:

    ❌ "Write code"
    ✅ "Write a Python function that takes a list of numbers and returns the median value"

    ❌ "Help with marketing"
    ✅ "Create 5 social media post ideas for a sustainable fashion brand targeting Gen Z"
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Provide context" icon="book">
    Give the AI relevant background information:

    - Your skill level (beginner, intermediate, expert)
    - What you've already tried
    - Specific constraints or requirements
    - The end goal you're trying to achieve

    **Example**: "I'm a beginner learning React. I tried using useState but got an error about hooks being called conditionally. Here's my code: [code]. How do I fix this?"
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Break down complex tasks" icon="list-check">
    For complex projects, break them into smaller steps:

    1. Start with a high-level plan
    2. Work through each step one at a time
    3. Ask follow-up questions to refine
    4. Iterate based on the AI's suggestions

    The AI works best when tackling focused, well-defined problems.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use the right model for the job" icon="wrench">
    Different models excel at different tasks:

    - **Coding**: GPT-5.3 Codex, Grok Code Fast 1
    - **Reasoning**: Claude Opus 4.6, GPT-5.2
    - **Speed**: GPT-oss 20b, Gemini 3 Flash
    - **Latest info**: Any model with web search enabled

    Don't be afraid to switch models mid-conversation if you need different capabilities.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Leverage web search wisely" icon="globe">
    Enable web search when you need:
    - Current events or recent news
    - Latest documentation versions
    - Real-time data (stock prices, weather, etc.)
    - Information from specific websites

    Disable it for:
    - Creative writing
    - Code generation (unless you need latest syntax)
    - General knowledge questions
    - Faster responses

    <Tip>
      Web search adds latency to responses. Only enable it when you specifically need current information.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Iterate and refine" icon="arrows-spin">
    Don't expect perfection on the first try:

    - Review the AI's response
    - Ask for clarification or changes
    - Request different approaches
    - Provide feedback on what you liked or didn't like

    **Example follow-ups**:
    - "Can you make this more concise?"
    - "Explain the technical details in simpler terms"
    - "Show me an alternative approach"
    - "Add error handling to this code"
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Messages aren't sending" icon="circle-exclamation">
    **Possible causes:**
    - Network connection issues
    - Rate limits exceeded
    - Invalid file attachments
    - Model temporarily unavailable

    **Solutions:**
    1. Check your internet connection
    2. Wait a few minutes and try again
    3. Try a different model
    4. Remove file attachments and try plain text
    5. Refresh the page

    <Info>
      If you're using a free account, you may have usage limits. Check **Settings** → **Usage** to see your current limits.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't see my conversations" icon="eye-slash">
    **If you're in guest mode:**
    - Guest conversations are stored in your browser only
    - Clearing browser data will delete them
    - Create a free account to save conversations permanently

    **If you have an account:**
    - Make sure you're signed in
    - Check that you're viewing the correct organization (if using teams)
    - Try refreshing the page
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Images or videos not generating" icon="image">
    **Common issues:**
    - Image/video mode not enabled
    - Model doesn't support image/video generation
    - Prompt doesn't clearly describe visual content
    - Service temporarily unavailable

    **Solutions:**
    1. Verify image/video mode is toggled on
    2. Switch to a model that supports generation
    3. Make your prompt more descriptive and specific
    4. Try again in a few minutes
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Web search not working" icon="magnifying-glass">
    **Check these items:**
    - Web search toggle is enabled
    - Your query requires current information
    - Brave Search API is configured (for self-hosted)

    <Note>
      Web search may not activate if the AI determines it can answer from its training data. Try asking explicitly: "Search the web for..."
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
